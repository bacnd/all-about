Fukushima

Discover Samurai Spirit & Outdoor Thrills

Journey north of Tokyo to Fukushima Prefecture, the gateway to Japan’s northeastern region of Tohoku. There you’ll find the samurai spirit alive and well, and have the opportunity to explore one of the most enduring aspects of Japanese history. You’ll also find unspoiled nature home to a wealth of thrilling outdoor activities.

JR EAST Pass (Tohoku)

Day 2

The JR EAST Pass (Tohoku) is a 5-day discount pass that allows for unlimited travel on JR trains and Shinkasen in an area of coverage from Tokyo to Aomori, which makes it the perfect way to explore the Tohoku region. Not only will you be able to conveniently travel to and around Fukushima, but you also have the option of going even further afield to see what else this exciting region has to offer.
