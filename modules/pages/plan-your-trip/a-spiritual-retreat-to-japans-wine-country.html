YAMANASHI & NAGANO

A Spiritual Retreat to Japan’s Wine Country

Central Japan is home to some truly breathtaking, unspoiled landscapes just begging to be explored. As such, it is a mecca for those looking to enjoy the great outdoors, especially Yamanashi Prefecture’s wine country, and the majestic peaks found in Nagano. It is also home to many sacred sites that can help visitors get in touch with Japanese spiritual traditions.

For this course, we recommend utilizing the JR East Pass (Nagano, Niigata) for travel. With this affordable pass, visitors can enjoy unlimited rides on all JR trains in its designated area, including shinkansen bullet trains, as well as the Narita Express and Tokyo Monorail. The pass also allows you to avoid the muss and fuss of purchasing individual tickets; just show your pass to station attendants at JR ticket gates and hop aboard!
