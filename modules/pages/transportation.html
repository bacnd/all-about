JR east

If you truly want to wander in and around Tokyo, there’s no better way to get around than with JR EAST. With an incredible rail network that extends from Tokyo to Hokkaido, and all the prefectures in between, there are a variety of useful and affordable passes to suit your travel needs. Whether you’re zooming from prefecture to prefecture on Shinkansen bullet trains, or slowly roaming on quaint local lines, JR EAST makes it easy to make the most of your time and money while you explore the Land of the Rising Sun.

Consider the following passes if you plan on traveling by Shinkansen

  • JR TOKYO Wide Pass
  • JR EAST Pass - Tohoku
  • JR EAST Pass - Nagano, Niigata
  • 2020 Pass

JR TOKYO Wide Pass

The JR TOKYO Wide Pass is a discounted pass for unlimited rides in Tokyo and the surrounding Kanto area. This 3-day pass is good for unlimited rides using reserved seats in ordinary cars on the Shinkansen, as well as limited express trains, and trains to and from Narita and Haneda airports.

JR EAST Pass(Tohoku)

The JR EAST Pass (Tohoku) is a discount 5-day pass that allows for unlimited travel on JR trains and Shinkansen in an area of coverage from Tokyo to Aomori, the northernmost prefecture in the Tohoku region. This pass makes it easy to explore northeastern Japan.

Get Ticket Info / Buy Ticket

JR EAST Pass(Nagano,Niigata)

The JR EAST Pass (Nagano, Niigata) is a discount 5-day pass that allows for unlimited travel on JR trains and Shinkansen in an area of coverage that extends from Tokyo to Nagano and Niigata prefectures. Explore the many unique charms of Nagano and Niigata prefectures without having to worry about purchasing tickets ahead of time!

Get Ticket Info / Buy Ticket

JR EAST RAIL PASS 2020

The JR EAST Welcome Rail Pass 2020 is a limited-time offer discount rail pass recommended for international visitors to Japan. It can be used for three consecutive days any time from October 16, 2020 to February 28, 2021. It allows for unlimited travel in a coverage area that spans from Tokyo to Aomori, as well as Niigata and Nagano prefectures.

Consider this pass for flexible travel around the Greater Tokyo area

The Welcome Suica pass from JR EAST makes traveling in and around Greater Tokyo a breeze. Valid on JR EAST trains, as well as subways and buses, visitors can avoid the hassle of purchasing individual tickets to your destination. Just tap your card at the ticket gate and go! Welcome Suica can be loaded with additional funds as needed, and is valid for 28 days of use.
