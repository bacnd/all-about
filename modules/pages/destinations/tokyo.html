Tokyo

As the beating heart of Japan, it’s certain that there’s no city in the world quite like Tokyo! A sprawling metropolis that combines modern ingenuity with traditional values, neon-lit cityscapes with contemplative green spaces, and has something new to explore around every corner, Tokyo is the ideal place to start any journey in Japan.
