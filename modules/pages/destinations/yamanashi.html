Yamanashi

Yamanashi is one of the prefectural homes of Mt. Fuji, and there’s a wealth of experiences to enjoy in the shadow of this epic Japanese icon. The area is Japan’s premier wine country, and in addition to a tipple and tour of vineyards, travelers can visit orchards to pick luscious fruits right off the vine!
