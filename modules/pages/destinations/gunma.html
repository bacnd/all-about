Gunma

Gunma Prefecture is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers! Located in the center of Japan’s main island and surrounded by mountains, its slopes are perfect for skiing in winter, or canyoneering and hiking in warmer months. It is also home to some historic hot spring villages, which are great places to enjoy a relaxing soak.
