Completely encompassed by the breathtaking Japanese Alps, the Echigo-Yuzawa region showcases Japan’s onsen culture at its finest. We’ve got the scoop to get you out the door and into the soothing hot springs of Niigata.

Rejuvenate yourself with the mineral-rich local spring water of Niigata, just a few hours from Tokyo.

After a seamless 90-minute bullet train ride from Tokyo Station, you’ll be transported into the depths of the spectacular Japanese Alps. Forget long walks, tiresome bus rides, or getting lost, you’ll be deep in hot spring territory the moment you step off the train. Besides the onsen inside the station, dozens of these naturally heated hot springs, many of which are within walking distance from the station, are filled with mineral-rich local spring water that will wash away fatigue, deep-clean pores, and rejuvenate your mind and body!

Nestled at the base of the Yuzawa Kogen Alps, many of Echigo-Yuzawa’s onsen sit upon the mountainside, providing bathers with endless panoramic views. Most onsen have traditional ryokan inns attached, so you can choose to spend the night in luxury. Being right in the heart of Japan’s most fertile rice region, Echigo-Yuzawa’s unique cuisine culture is not to be missed. Pair some mouthwatering regional dishes like hegi soba noodles with a local sake for the ultimate Niigata gourmet experience. After your dinner, enjoy a quick pick-me-up by using one of the several free footbaths dotted about town.

While the spectacular frozen scenery and perfect ski conditions make Echigo-Yuzawa synonymous with winter, the area is worth visiting any time of the year. The lush greenery of summer combined with the high altitude makes it a refreshing escape from the mid-year humidity of Kanto, full of fun outdoor activities. Later on, stunning fall foliage will transform the mountains into fiery photogenic gradients of color. The temperature here drops quickly, allowing travelers to recommence their love with onsen warmth as early as September. Echigo-Yuzawa is a true year-round destination!

Echigo-Yuzawa Onsen

Less than two hours away from Tokyo you’ll find some of the most relaxing onsen hot spring resorts in the nation, right in the middle of the snow! Head to Echigo-Yuzawa to experience thrills, chills, and the winter feels.
