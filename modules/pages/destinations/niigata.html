Niigata

As one of Japan’s heartlands for rice cultivation, Niigata is perhaps best known for its cuisine and sake. Depending on the season, visitors can also feast their eyes on picture-perfect terraced rice fields, scenic beaches, or the snow-filled ski slopes of Echigo-Yuzawa. There’s nothing like a dip in a hot spring after a day enjoying hiking, or the myriad other outdoor activities available.
