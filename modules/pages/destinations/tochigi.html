Tochigi

Tochigi offers visitors the chance to experience the way Japan can seamlessly blend the beauty of nature with enduring spiritual traditions. Whether exploring the UNESCO-recognized temples and shrines of Nikko, hiking around Japan’s highest natural lake, Lake Chuzenji, or relaxing at the hot spring resorts of Kinugawa Onsen, Tochigi has something to offer all types of travelers.
