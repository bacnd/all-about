Saitama

As its nearby northern neighbor, Saitama is easily accessible from Tokyo. The prefecture is characterized by its historic towns like Kawagoe, which maintain a retro atmosphere straight out of the Edo era. It is also remarkable for its mountainous peaks and lush river valleys, full of excellent hikes, outdoor activities, and seasonal flowers and foliage.
