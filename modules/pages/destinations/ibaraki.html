Ibaraki

Ibaraki is perhaps best known for its flower-filled parks—especially Hitachi Seaside Park—and traditional gardens, which can be enjoyed throughout the year. It is also the location of the towering, twin-peaked Mt. Tsukuba, which is a mecca for hikers. The expansive coast offers lovely seaside views, and towns like Koga provide a tranquil, temple-filled atmosphere.
